Odin's Mango Tango

Description

This mango tango juice drink is served over ice for fantastic summertime refreshment!

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Total: 10 mins
  • Servings: 2
  • Yield: 2 glasses

Ingedients

  • 2 medium mangos, peeled and sliced
  • 2 apples, cored and quartered
  • 2 peeled limes

Directions

  1. Juice the mango, apples, and limes in a juice machine.
  2. Serve over ice.
